Welcome to the unofficial Gridfinity site!

This is very much a work in progress. Come join us on #gridfinity in Zack's discord to help!

Gridfinity could be your workshop's ultimate modular storage system to keep you productive and organized. It is free, open source, and almost 100% 3D printable.

Alexander Chappels Assortment System, licensed under Creative Commons - Attribution - Non-Commercial - Share Alike, partly inspired Zack Freedman's initial designs of Gridfinity. The Gridfinity designs were first released in the video "Gridfinity: Your Ultimate Modular Workshop is FREE!" as a framework for the community to extend.

Now Gridfinity is in the hands of a thriving community that continually adapts it to their needs. We invite you to join this community by using and adapting the system!
